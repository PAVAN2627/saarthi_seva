About Saarthi Seva

Saarthi Seva is a community-powered platform connecting people across rural and urban India for ride-sharing and essential assistance. Whether you're offering a ride by bike, car, or traveller, or seeking help with medicine or deliveries, we make it seamless and reliable. Our mission is to empower communities with safe, affordable, and accessible transportation and support, driven by user ratings and real-time updates.

Our Services

  • Ride-sharing across rural and urban areas (bike, car, traveller).
  • Post and find rides effortlessly with real-time updates.
  • Request essentials like medicines and daily goods.
  • Community-rated system for safety and reliability.
  • Simple and accessible interface for all users.
